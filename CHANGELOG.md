[1.1.4]

With this update, most biomes received a range of tweaks and enhancements. The addition of several smaller sub-biomes helps make the world feel more immersive and exciting to explore, adding a richer sense of realism to every journey.

**_Note:_**
_The release channel has been temporarily moved back to **Beta** due to massive world generation changes in this update._

**Added**
* You can now find twigs and pebbles laying on the ground in certain biomes. Use them to craft cobblestone or sticks.
* Cypress Wood Set
* Marlstone Stone Set
* Fen Moss - you can use it to transform dirt into clay

**Changed**
* Reworked Floating Leaves Texture
* Reworked Begonie Texture
* Reworked Laterite Stone Set Textures
* Reworked Fan Palm Log Textures
* Reworked Fir Window Textures
* Reworked most Leaves Textures
* Reworked Quicksand Texture
* Reworked Marsh Texture
* Reworked Travertine Stone Set Textures
* Adjusted saturation for most flowers
* Forest Moss doesnt slow players anymore
* Forest Moss can now be used to generate mushrooms
* Rebalanced Wandering Gardener Trades

**Fixed**
* Swamp Oak, Fan Palm and Orange Leaves loottable

**Biome Changes**

_General Biome Changes_
* Coarse and rooted dirt patches were added across multiple biomes to improve terrain variation
* Tall grass density was adjusted biome-by-biome for a cleaner look
* Stone boulders were added to certain biomes
* Flower patch generation was reworked to a noise-based system for a more organic, less cluttered distribution

_Plains_
* Reworked tree distribution

_Sunflower Plains_
* Added new flowers to flower patches
* Sunflowers now follow the direction of the sun

_River_
* Cattail and Reed patches now spawn along river beds
* Adjusted travertine distribution
* Adjusted tree distribution

_Forest_
* Flower patches now primarily generate in natural clearings

_Flower Forest_
* Uses the new noise-based flower distribution

_Birch Forest_
* Uses the updated noise-based flower generation

_Old Growth Birch Forest_
* Re-added

_Dark Forest_
* Flower generation was adjusted to better fit the darker atmosphere

_Taiga_
* Reworked tree shapes

_Old Growth Spruce Taiga_
* Reworked tree shapes
* Re-added

_Jungle_
* Reworked tree shapes
* Adjusted tree distribution
* Adjusted floating leaves distribution

_Sparse Jungle_
* Reworked tree shapes
* Adjusted tree distribution
* Adjusted floating leaves distribution

_Desert_
* Added Desert Lily - a really rare flower

_Swamp_
* Reworked tree shapes
* Adjusted tree distribution


**New Biomes**

_Brushland_
* A subbiome to the Savanna, covered in bushes and grass patches

_Baobab Savanna_
* A subbiome to the Savanna, baobab trees only spawn in this new biome
* Mostly permeated by dark coarse patches

_Aspen Forest_
* Now its own biome

_Cold Grassland_
* A subbiome to the Taiga, plains-like but with firs instead of oak trees

_Cypress Fields_
* A completely new biome and subbiome to the Savanna
* Cypress Trees and Marlstone can be found here

_Desert Oasis_
* A really rare subbiome to the Desert with small water sources and palm trees

_Fen_
* Swamp-like biome spawning in colder areas
* Under the surface you can find lots of clay

_Forest Edge_
* Sometimes spawning around forests, breaking up sharp transitions from Forests to Plains or other Biomes

_Larch Forest_
* Now its own biome

_Flower Glade / Golden Glade_
* Clearings spawning inside forests, mostly with more flowers and denser vegetation

_Rivers_
* Taigas, Deserts and Jungles now have their own river variants so they dont break up the immersion

_Marshland_
* Swamp-like biome spawning near other swamps but with less trees

_Highland Woods_
* New cool-temperate woodland biome bridging plains and colder regions
* Features spruce, oak and birch with a more open, natural structure
